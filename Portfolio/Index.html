Mi'kmaq Game

As part of my 2023 Mobile App Development course project at Saint Mary's University, I joined a team of four on a fun quest to create an educational computer game for young Mi'kmaq learners. We embarked on this adventure armed with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and tons of creativity!
Our mission was to make learning Mi'kmaq words a fun and engaging experience for children ages 5-6. We crafted a game using drag-and-drop technology and a visually appealing Bootstrap grid system, ensuring that every element of the game was both educational and entertaining.
Together, we brought our vision to life, creating a game that not only taught young learners Mi'kmaq words but also sparked their curiosity and love for language. It was an incredibly rewarding experience, and I'm proud to have been part of a team that made a positive impact on the community.
